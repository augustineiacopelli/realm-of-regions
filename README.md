# Realm of Regions

**A browser RPG designed and created by Simon Iacopelli.**

Built as a standalone web game based on Simon's hand-written design notes, including all enemy stats, region names, level progression, item shop, and boss encounters — every element comes directly from his original vision.

---

## How to Play

1. Enter your name and begin your adventure.
2. Travel through four regions: **Forest**, **Mountain**, **Lava**, and **Frost**.
3. Fight enemies to earn XP and gold, level up, and grow stronger.
4. Challenge each region's boss to unlock the next region.
5. Defeat the **Frost Wizard** — the final boss — to complete the game.

## Features

- **4 Regions** with unique enemies and bosses, each with their own HP and attack stats
- **Secret Bosses** in every region — defeat 3 enemies without dying to unlock them
- **Turn-based battle system** with Attack, Magic, Defend, Item, Act, and Spare actions
- **ACT mechanic** — learn about each enemy and make the enemy miss their turn
- **SPARE mechanic** — spare regular enemies after using ACT (bosses will destroy you for trying)
- **Level system** — 11 levels with a doubling XP curve; each level up restores full HP and grants +20 max HP
- **Item Store** — Potions, Swords, Chest Plates, Bagels, Hotdogs, Cheddar, and more
- **Inn** — rest for 15g to fully restore HP and MP
- **Save system** — progress saved to localStorage

## Regions & Bosses

| Region | Boss | Secret Boss |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Forest | Forest Wizard (36 HP) | Shadow Wizard (80 HP) |
| Mountain | Mountain Wizard (44 HP) | Storm Wizard (100 HP) |
| Lava | Flare Wizard (60 HP) | Inferno Wizard (120 HP) |
| Frost | Frost Wizard (250 HP) | Void Wizard (300 HP) |
